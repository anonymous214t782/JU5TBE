    BOOKS

    HOW TO THINK LIKE A ROMAN EMPEROR

    How to think like a roman emperor is about the of the roman emperor MARCUS AURELIUS who is one of possibly one of the most famous stoic philosopher. it retells the his life and the trials he had to go through and how he managent to deal with them.... Main concepts •Do not focus on things you cannot control. •Its not the situation that matters,but how you respond to it. •A life well lived is a life in which you strive for VIRTUE, JUSTICE, WISDOM, TEMPERANCE.

    POWER OF NOW

    The Power of now is a book that emphasizes being in the present moment (ties to Flow).Its a spritual book that promotes being self aware and not tying yourself to thoughts (ties to stocism).It also states unresolved past truama creates the "pain body" which creates negative thoughts and behaviours.... Main concepts •The only true moment is the now.It is also where inner peace resides. •The ego creates attachments to thoughts,ideas and possesions,living in the now destroys the ego. •Accept all that comes with equanimity( ties with stoicism).

    WIN YOUR INNER BATTLES

    Win your inner battles is a book about self control its divided into three parts that explore different but relatable ways to create self awareness and self control.... Main concepts •KNOW THYSELF ... NO like seriously know yourself (there is a spreedsheet you can use there). •BUILD CONFIDENCE WITH COMPETENCE (IMPORTANT!). •Find your purpose and live in the moment.

    EGO IS THE ENEMY

    this book (as the title implies) points out the dangers of having an ego,It creates a clear distinction between ego and confidence and also promotes humbleness as a way of destroying it.... Main concepts •Ego is when you need validation,confidence is tied with ability(CONFIDENCE by COMPETENCE). •Self awareness is key in identifying and destroying ego. •Know that you do not know and be willing to learn.

    FLOW

    Flow is a book that primarily focuses how we can find happiness in our lives. The book argues that for there to be happiness one must spend their time in flow... Main Concepts •Flow is the state in which pleasure is archieved whenyou dissociate with worldly worries and completely focus one the task or moment •Archieving a state of flow requires an activity in which (1. it has a skill level and its easy to gain feeback on actions 2. what you are doing is challenging enough to be exciting yet not too difficult to be overwhelming) •Regularily archieving this state can provide lifelong happiness and contentment even through normal chores.

    MEDITATIONS

    Meditations is based on the actual writings of Marcus Aurelius(was actually a journal) it details his life and his attempt to live a life of stoic virtue.... Main concepts •Its not the thing that hurts you but rather your perception of it. •Everyone should strive to live a life of virtue. •vices (e.g smoking,drinking) should be used be enjoyed moderately.
