    TRADING IN THE ZONE

    Trading in the zone is one of revolutionary books about trading, it focuses on the mental aspectsof trading and their impact. This is a must read if you want to trade,professionally gamble(yes thats a thing) invest (basically if you are dealing with probabilities) or just nje... Main concepts •Having high emotional intelligence mattters more than having an edge in trading •Once you have created a strong trading method then you should maintain discipline and follow its ruless accordingly •You have to consistently analyse (aka be AWARE) your performance both tradingwise and mentally

    THE WEALTH CHEF

    The wealth chef is a book that is similar to rich dad poor dad but takes a cooking approach to explaining its concepts.COOL...ish... •BEING AWARE!!!!!!!! your spending habits is imporant in order to handle your finances and develop a solid plan for your finances •Once your plan is created, Make sure that you stick to the plan in order for the compounding effect to help towards your financial goals(ties to the compound effect,atomic habits,the power of habit,rich dad poor dad etc) •your definition of wealth is important as it will shape your goals and how you plan to archieve those goals (e.if wealth to you is defined as not having to work for a living then wealth to you is a good passive income)

    RICH DAD POOR DAD

    Rich dad poor dad (must read btw)is a book(moreso biography) about Robrert Kyosaki's childhood,the book shines a contrast on how beliefs and discussions about money differ between rich and poor families.... Main concepts •The book emphasizes understanding money like rich people do do,it argues that the subject of money isn't really taught in schools and is something you have to consistently learn about yourself. •It also brings a new definition of asset.Being that an asset brings money while a liability takes money (e.g a house *if you dont rent it out* is a liability since it consistently drains money from your wallet in maintainence).This means that to create wealth you must invest in assets while also taking calculated risks in the path of financial freedom. •

    MARKET WIZARD

    This book contains interviews from some of the most successful traders in the industry, in interview format... •The most important thing in trading (moreso in life overall)is risk management aka(not losing all your money in a single trade) (ties to trading in the zone and GAME THEORY) •Be a student for life,the market changes so always be learning (kinda like life) •Don't be moody...(control your enmotions,lest they control you) KINDA LIKE LIFE!(ties to a lot of philosophies....SOOOOO IMPORTANT)

    THE PSYCHOLOGY OF MONEY

    IT is in the title really,kinda like RICH DAD and a good book to learn about common misconceptions about money... Main Concepts •Your daily actions determine your financial success (ties to rich dad poor dad),therefore small wise decisions daily overtime lead to wealth •Financial freedom is the ultimate form of wealth and •Understand the level of financial wealth comfortable to you (the point where most of what you desire financially is reached....it differs)
